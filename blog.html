How to manage a home garden

A healthy garden requires regular maintenance throughout the growing season. Most plants require sun, water and nutrients to produce well. Gardeners must mainly worry about nutrients and water if they plant in a bed with the proper sun exposure.

How plants affect mental health

Studies show that all plants provide mental health benefits. Constantly seeing and being around plants help us feel more calm and relaxed, thus decreasing levels of anxiety. Nurturing and caring for our plants can also reduce stress levels and boost our mood.
